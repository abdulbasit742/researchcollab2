# RCollab — Global Expansion & Localization Blueprint

## Expansion Phases

### Phase 1: Regional Dominance (Pre-Expansion)
Gates: 90%+ institutional retention, 80%+ sponsor repeat, <5% dispute, 99.9% uptime, stable unit economics, positive cash flow, escrow stable 6+ months. If not achieved → NO EXPANSION.

### Phase 2: Adjacent Region Entry
Select based on: similar education structure, similar legal environment, English compatibility, strong engineering schools, innovation ecosystem maturity.

### Phase 3: Multi-Region Infrastructure
Global reporting dashboard, multi-region analytics, cross-region sponsor collaboration, international benchmarking.

### Phase 4: Cross-Region Collaboration
Cross-border sponsor funding, multi-institution international projects, multi-currency escrow, international milestone compliance.

### Phase 5: Global Infrastructure Position
National-level analytics, international funding statistics, cross-border innovation collaboration.

## Regulatory Preparation Per Region
Legal review of escrow regulations, payment processing compliance, data privacy, institutional contracting, KYC requirements, tax reporting. Never launch without compliance map.

## Multi-Currency Architecture
Multi-currency wallet support, conversion transparency, region-specific escrow accounts, separate regional reporting, exchange rate risk management, ledger tagging by region.

## Expansion KPI Gates
Before next region: 12 months stability, 85%+ institutional retention, 70%+ gross margin, strong sponsor repeat, no major escrow incidents. No skipping gates.
