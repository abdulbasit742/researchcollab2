# RCollab — Revenue Model & Unit Economics

## 10 Revenue Streams

1. **Transaction Fee** — 2-8% sliding scale on escrow, lower for institutions
2. **Institutional Subscription** — Starter / Professional / Enterprise tiers
3. **Sponsor Premium Analytics** — ROI tracking, portfolio analytics
4. **Verified Badge Fees** — Institution, sponsor, faculty verification
5. **Compliance & KYC Fee** — Processing for verification and tax docs
6. **Enterprise SLA Contracts** — Guaranteed escrow/dispute SLAs
7. **API Access Licensing** — Free limited, paid advanced
8. **Aggregated Data Insights** — Anonymous academic funding trends
9. **Featured Listings** — Highlighted sponsor projects, priority university listing
10. **Strategic Partnerships** — Revenue sharing with universities, government, corporates

## Unit Economics Targets
- CAC < LTV by 3x
- Dispute rate < 5%
- 80%+ sponsor retention
- 85%+ project completion
- 90%+ institutional retention
- 99.9% uptime

## 3-Year Financial Projection
- Year 1: Small institutional base, low fee, proof of trust, modest revenue
- Year 2: Subscription scaling, increased GMV, stronger margins
- Year 3: Regional expansion, enterprise contracts, API licensing, analytics revenue

## Pricing Discipline
Before changing pricing, evaluate: impact on trust, institutional retention, dispute frequency, sponsor adoption, competitive comparison.
