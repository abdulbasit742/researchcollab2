# RCollab — Competitive War Strategy

## Battlefield Definition
Category: "Escrow-Backed Academic Execution Infrastructure" — not freelancer marketplace, not social network, not gig platform.

## Positioning Control
- Upwork = Freelancers | LinkedIn = Identity | Handshake = Hiring | ResearchGate = Publication
- **RCollab = Funded Execution**
- "Others show profiles. We execute funded projects safely."

## Feature Superiority Matrix
- Escrow: RCollab > Upwork
- Institutional Dashboard: RCollab > All
- Verified Execution History: RCollab > LinkedIn
- Academic Integration: RCollab > Handshake
- Funding + Milestones: RCollab > ResearchGate
- Compliance + Audit: RCollab > Fiverr

## Market Segmentation
Target ONLY: Universities, engineering schools, tech departments, innovation labs, R&D sponsors.
Avoid: General freelance, mass consumer gig, influencer growth.

## Pricing Discipline
Justify fee through trust, institutional embedding, audit & compliance. Never undercut aggressively.

## Institutional Capture (5 Steps)
1. Embed in FYP workflow
2. Embed in sponsor engagement
3. Embed in reporting
4. Integrate via API
5. Become operational dependency

## Defensive Strategy
- If Upwork enters academic niche → emphasize university integrations, faculty tools, compliance
- If LinkedIn adds escrow → emphasize immutable ledger, milestone enforcement, dispute SLA
- Stay infrastructure-focused

## War Metrics
Institutional retention, sponsor repeat rate, escrow reliability %, completion rate, dispute rate, execution reputation adoption, workflow integration depth.
