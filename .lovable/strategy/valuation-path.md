# RCollab — Path to $1B Valuation

## Valuation Drivers
ARR, growth rate, gross margin, retention rate, market size, defensibility, predictability, infrastructure positioning.
Infrastructure companies: 8x–25x ARR.

## Phase 1: Trust & Foundation (Years 0–2)
- 50–100 universities, 2,000+ funded projects, 85%+ completion, <5% dispute, 90%+ retention
- $2–5M ARR → $20M–$50M valuation

## Phase 2: Regional Dominance (Years 2–4)
- 300+ universities, 20,000+ projects, $30M–$50M ARR, 90% retention, 80% sponsor repeat
- $300M–$600M valuation

## Phase 3: Multi-Region Expansion (Years 4–6)
- 1,000+ universities, 100,000+ projects, $80M–$120M ARR, 70%+ gross margin
- $800M–$1.5B valuation

## Phase 4: Infrastructure Status (Years 6–10)
- National partnerships, government collaboration, $150M–$250M ARR
- At 8x–15x ARR: $1.2B–$3.7B valuation

## Path to $100M+ ARR
500 universities × $200k average = $100M ARR. Realistic at global scale.

## Required Metrics at Scale
- ARR growth > 40% YoY
- Net Revenue Retention > 110%
- Gross margin > 65%
- CAC payback < 12 months
- Dispute rate < 5%
- Completion rate > 80%
