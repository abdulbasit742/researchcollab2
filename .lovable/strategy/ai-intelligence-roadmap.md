# RCollab — AI & Data Intelligence Roadmap

## Core Rule
AI must NOT control money, bypass invariants, or execute escrow logic. AI assists, never replaces financial core.

## 10 Phases

### 1. Data Foundation (Years 1–2)
Structure and clean data: project lifecycle timestamps, milestone duration, approval speed, dispute occurrence, sponsor repeat rate, escrow funding patterns. No AI until data is reliable.

### 2. Intelligence Layer (Assistive Only)
Flag slow milestones, detect at-risk projects, highlight high-performing institutions, identify churn signals, suggest intervention alerts. Recommendations only, not automatic actions.

### 3. Predictive Risk Layer
Risk scoring: past disputes, milestone delays, communication inactivity, sponsor behavior, institutional performance. Flag "High Risk" — never block automatically.

### 4. Matching Optimization
Sponsor-student matching using verified execution history (not social signals). Best teams, similar past successes, strongest track records, skill-based matching.

### 5. Institutional Analytics
AI-powered dashboards: completion heatmaps, faculty performance, sponsor satisfaction trends, funding concentration, milestone velocity comparisons.

### 6. Reputation Intelligence
Enhanced ERS: time-based reliability, improvement trajectory, risk-adjusted trust, performance consistency. Explainable scores, no black boxes.

### 7. Automation Assistance
Milestone breakdown suggestions, risk warnings before funding, SLA breach predictions, dispute resolution support, compliance reminders. AI assists, humans approve.

### 8. Governance & Safety
AI never moves funds/changes escrow/updates ledger. Outputs logged and auditable. Bias monitoring. Transparent scoring logic.

### 9. Data Defensibility
Build largest structured dataset of escrow-backed academic execution history. Non-replicable historical execution graph.

### 10. Long-Term Infrastructure Position
Academic Execution Intelligence Infrastructure: national funding trends, innovation velocity, employability forecasting, institutional benchmarking.
