# RCollab — Investor Pitch Architecture (Series A Ready)

## 15-Slide Structure

### Slide 1: The Problem
Universities lack structured execution infrastructure for funded academic projects. FYPs managed via spreadsheets, no escrow protection, no milestone enforcement, no audit trail, no sponsor-university pipeline.

### Slide 2: The Solution
Escrow-backed academic execution infrastructure: structured milestone workflow, immutable ledger, sponsor protection, institutional dashboards, faculty oversight, verified execution history, compliance-ready audit export.

### Slide 3: Why Now
Universities under pressure to improve employability, corporate demand for practical experience, remote collaboration growth, need for funding transparency, institutional digital transformation.

### Slide 4: Market Size
- TAM: Global university capstone/FYP market + corporate R&D collaboration
- SAM: Engineering, tech, business schools
- SOM (Year 3): 50–200 universities regionally

### Slide 5: Product
Escrow funding flow, milestone approval, ledger verification, university dashboard, sponsor dashboard, reputation engine.

### Slide 6: Competition
- Upwork: No institutional layer
- LinkedIn: Identity, not execution
- Handshake: Hiring, not funding
- ResearchGate: Visibility, not capital
- RCollab: Escrow-backed institutional execution infrastructure

### Slide 7: Moat
Immutable execution history, institutional embedding, escrow trust, compliance readiness, SLA tracking, execution graph, university workflow integration, network effects.

### Slide 8: Business Model
2–8% escrow fee, institutional subscriptions, sponsor premium analytics, enterprise SLA contracts, API licensing, aggregated analytics.

### Slide 9: Traction
Universities onboarded, projects completed, GMV, completion rate, dispute rate, escrow reliability, sponsor retention.

### Slide 10: Unit Economics
CAC, LTV, average escrow value, gross margin, churn rate, infrastructure cost per transaction. Target: LTV > 3x CAC.

### Slide 11: 3-Year Roadmap
- Year 1: Trust + escrow perfection
- Year 2: Institutional embedding + revenue scaling
- Year 3: Regional expansion + analytics

### Slide 12: Risk Mitigation
Financial correctness, regulatory exposure, institutional adoption friction, competitive entry, scalability. Test suite + invariant enforcement + monitoring.

### Slide 13: Team
Technical competence, security-first mindset, institutional understanding, long-term discipline.

### Slide 14: Vision
The infrastructure layer for academic execution. Not marketplace. Infrastructure.

### Slide 15: The Ask
Funding amount, runway, milestones, growth targets, institutional expansion goals. Tied to escrow trust scaling.

## Positioning
Never say: "We are LinkedIn + Upwork + ResearchGate."
Say: "We are the escrow-backed execution infrastructure layer universities never had."
