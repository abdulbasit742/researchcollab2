# RCollab — 3-Year Execution Roadmap

## Year 1 — Foundation & Trust
**Goal:** Safest academic execution platform in region.

### Priority 1: Financial Core Perfection
- KPIs: 99.9% uptime, 0 escrow failures, 0 ledger corruption, 100% invariant coverage

### Priority 2: Clean Deal Lifecycle UX
- KPIs: Milestone approval < 48h, dispute resolution < 7 days, 80%+ completion rate

### Priority 3: Institutional Verification
- KPIs: 5-15 verified institutions, 100+ escrow-backed projects, 70% repeat sponsor rate

## Year 2 — Institutional Scale & Data Moat
**Goal:** Become institutional infrastructure.

### Priority 1: Reputation & Execution Graph
- KPIs: 500+ verified escrow projects, <5% dispute rate

### Priority 2: University OS Layer
- KPIs: 20-50 universities, 1,000+ students, 200+ sponsors

### Priority 3: Revenue Optimization
- KPIs: Sustainable revenue, CAC < LTV, positive unit economics

## Year 3 — Controlled Expansion
**Goal:** Expand carefully without breaking core.

### Priority 1: Innovation & Research Layer
### Priority 2: Advanced Analytics
### Priority 3: Cross-Region Expansion
- KPIs: 2-3 regions, 5,000+ projects, 90%+ escrow reliability, 85%+ completion rate

## Measurement Framework (Monthly)
GMV, Escrow reliability %, Dispute rate, Completion rate, Sponsor retention, Institutional retention, Avg milestone approval time, SLA compliance, Revenue growth, Unit economics

## Discipline Rule
Before any new major feature: 3 consecutive months stable uptime, no invariant failure, dispute rate below threshold, monitoring clean, positive sponsor retention.
